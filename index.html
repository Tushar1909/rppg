Remote Photoplethysmography (rPPG) Heartbeat Detection System

Welcome to our Remote Photoplethysmography (rPPG) Heartbeat Detection System! Our technology utilizes advanced image processing techniques to remotely measure your heartbeat without any physical contact.

Instructions: Position yourself in a well-lit environment, ensuring your face is clearly visible to the camera. For best results, try in a constantly well lit space with minimal device and subject motion.
